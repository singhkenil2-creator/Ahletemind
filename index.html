Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data we collect

AthleteMind stores all your data (name, training logs, nutrition, stats) locally on your device using localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to external servers unless you enable cloud sync.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising trackers
  • We do not require account creation

Your rights (GDPR)

You can delete all your data at any time via Profile → Clear All Data. This permanently removes everything from your device.

Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to save your preferences and training data locally.

Contact

Questions? Email us at contact@athletemind.app or use the feedback button in the app. Data controller: Kenil Singh.
